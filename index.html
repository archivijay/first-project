MediLL Products

We provide high quality, highly trustable and affordable medical products to assist in maintainance of one's sexual health. We, at MediLL leave no Gynecologial and Fertility related problems unseen.

ADDFOL

Addfol is an active form of Folic Acid, Vitamin B12 and B6, which are highly effective in Pregnancy and Lactation and Recurrent Early Pregnancy Losses.

ADDFOL D2

Addfol-D2 is a combination of Vitamin D & DHA along with active form of folic acid, Vitamin B12 and B6. Used in 2nd and 3rd trimesters of pregnancy.

ADDFOL NVP

Addfol-NVP is a Eustragic coated tablet with combination of Doxylamine and Folic Acid which is effective in morning sickness of pregnancy.

ARGINEW

Arginew is a sachet of L-Arginine, good in taste and increase placental blood flow during 2nd and 3rd trimesters of pregnancy.

ADDFERT - F

Addfert-F is a syngertic combination of drugs to treat Female-Infertility due to PCOS and Harmonal imbalances.

ADDFERT - M

Addfert-M has the highest amount of CQ10, L-Carnitine and Asthaxanthin which is devoted to rehabilitate and support Male-Fertility.

CALCI - 1K

Calci-1k is a calcium dosage having the highest concentration of elemental Calcium with highest Bio availability.

FERPREG

Ferpreg is the 4thgeneration of Iron suppliments, which leads to highest Haemoglobin rise without any side effects like Nausia and Vomiting.

ONELET - 2.5

Onelet-2.5 is a tablet of Letrozole for increase in follicular size and produce single ova for ovulation.

P - 200 SR

P is for Progesterone which remains in the body for upto 30 hours, serves in maintaining Pregnancy's Leutal Phase Deffects.

P - 300 SR

P is for Progesterone which remains in the body for upto 30 hours, serves in maintaining Pregnancy's Leutal Phase Deffects.

ROBOTIC

Robotic is a tablet in capsule coating of Rabiprazole & Domperidone for Acute Gastroenteritis.
